INTRODUCTION

AIT ENGINEERING SERVICES-ISLAMABAD established as an independent firm provides services in the field of Civil/Electrical engineering. The firm has now established a state of the art Geo tech/Quality testing laboratory in Islamabad. The laboratory is equipped with latest technology and expert Engineers/technicians. The facility is capable of conducting In situ soil investigation, all types of lab test and quality testing for roads/buildings to the satisfaction of Clients.

AIM

To provide authentic and genuine soil investigation data and ensure quality construction through accurate testing without compromising the results.

POLICY

AIT Engineering Services is committed to achieve and maintain high level and standard of quality in all its field operations meeting the requirements of the client and ensure precise lab testing. Measurement operations will comply with the requirements of latest test standards. All test reports are professionally documented and prior to issue are again audited. Finalized test reports are routinely saved as pdf files for storage and flexible client transfer, and can be provided as hard copy (by post and/or fax), electronic transfer or both whenever required.
